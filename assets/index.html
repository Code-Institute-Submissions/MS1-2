Our patient Journey

Consultation.Review.Pre-Op.Surgery.AfterCare.FabulousYou

Upon recieving your initial enquiry, you will recieve a telephone call from one of our Aesthetic Nurses. When we have established the procedures you are considering, you will then meet with your surgeon to map out your vision. You will then be booked in for a Pre-Operation appointment a day or two before surgery. You will recieve information on how best to make a speedy recovery. depending on which procedure you choose, the down time, and overnight stay (if needed) will be included.
