Our patient Journey

Consultation. Review. Proceedure. AfterCare. FabulousYou

Upon recieving your initial enquiry, you will recieve a telephone call from one of our Aesthetic Nurses. When we have established the procedures you are considering, you will then meet with your Aesthetic Consultant, together we will map out your vision, and check your suitability with the products we use. You will then be booked in for an appointment for your proceedure, (most cases take less then an hour). You will recieve information on aftercare and how best to make a speedy recovery. Due to the nature of our procedures the down time, is minimal. Swelling is expected and true results will be visable after 3-5 days.
