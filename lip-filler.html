Lip Filler

Other name: Lip augmentation

Cost: Â£250

Procedure time: 15-30mins

Dermal fillers are also known as 'Hyaluronic acid' or 'Calcium hydroxyapatite'. The filler is injected under the skin into soft tissue to define and add volume to the lips. Most people do this to achieve a plumper or a fuller pout; others use this to even out the appearance of their lips.

These fillers are not a permanent fix and require a top up every 12 months.

Bruising and swelling may occur for 3-5 days.
